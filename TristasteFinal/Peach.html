  Peach Yoghurt Parfait

Peach parfait is a delicate summer dessert which is very easy to make. It has a refreshing flavor and multiple layers.

Cooking time: 20 minutes
Serving: 1
Calories: <200 kcal

Ingredients:
Peach 1/2 
Granola 3 tbsp
Plain yoghurt 3 tbsp 
Castella cake 1 slice

Steps:

1. Cut the castella cake into small cubes. Place it at the bottom of the parfait cup.

2. Press evenly over the castella cake using a spoon. Put the plain yoghurt and granola on top.

3. Cut the peach into slices and place it around the edge of the parfait cup. Ready to serve.
