  Red Wine Vinegar Baguette

Looking for party food recipes? Red wine vinegar seasoned cherry tomatoes paired with crispy French bread shall win the hearts of your guests!

Cooking time: 30 minutes
Serving: 3
Calories: <300 kcal per serving

General Ingredients:
Sliced French bread 6 
Butter Q.S. 
Garlic 2 cloves 
Cream Cheese appropriate amount 
Cherry tomatoes 18 

Salad Dressing:
Red wine vinegar 1 tbsp 
Olive oil 2 tbsp 
Honey 1 tbsp
Italian mixed herbs Q.S. 
Black pepper appropriate amount
Salt 1 pinch

Steps:

1. Divide each cherry tomato into 4 pieces.

2. Mix all ingredients of the salad dressing. Add cherry tomatoes and mix well.

3. Butter both sides of the French bread and grill until golden brown.

4. Rub one side of the French bread with a clove of garlic.

5. Spread cream cheese on the French bread and put on the seasoned cherry tomatoes. Ready to serve.
