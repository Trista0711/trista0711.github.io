  Fig & Cheese Penne Pasta

A flavourful dish with the combination of sweet-and-sour figs and smoky bacon and cheese.

Cooking time: 40 minutes
Serving: 3
Calories: <400 kcal per serving

General Ingredients:
Penne pasta 180 g
Bacon 4 slice 
Fig 5 
Mixed salad leaves Q.S. 
Goat cheese Q.S. 

Pasta sauce:
Olive oil 80 g 
Lemon juice 60 g 
Minced garlic Q.S. 
Salt 1 pinch 
Black pepper appropriate amount

Steps:

1. Cut the bacon into pieces. Chop the goat cheese. Cut each fig into 4 pieces.

2. Bring a pot of water to boil. Put in penne pasta and cook for 10-12 minutes. Drain the pasta.

3. Heat the pan at medium-heat. Stir fry the bacon for 5 minutes.

4. In a big bowl, add in olive oil, lemon juice, minced garlic, salt and pepper. 

5. Mix well to make the pasta sauce. Add in penne pasta, bacon, figs and mixed salad leaves. Mix well.

6. Sprinkle goat cheese on top. Ready to serve.
