About Tristaste

My name is Trista. I am enthusiastic about cooking and baking. “Tristaste” is a portmanteau word comprised of my name “Trista” and “taste”, which represents my passion as well as my cooking style. It is a media company specialized in creating low-calorie recipes and video demonstrations for people who are calorie-conscious but still want to enjoy good food, which is consistent with our brand’s core values: Healthy and Yummy!
